The Problem

The old way is broken.

College athletics scheduling hasn't changed in decades. Coaches are still drowning in spreadsheets, group texts, and compliance anxiety.

📋

Manual schedule conflicts

Coaches waste hours cross-referencing class schedules, syllabi, and practice windows just to find a time that works for everyone.

Average time wasted: 4–6 hrs/week
⚠️

NCAA violations happen

CARA hour limits are hard to track manually across a full roster. One mistake can trigger NCAA sanctions that derail your entire program.

Up to $5,000 fine per violation
📱

Athletes miss the memo

Practice time changes get lost in group texts and email threads. Half the team shows up late or not at all, wasting everyone's time.

67% of athletes miss at least 1 update/week

Athletes sync their schedule
